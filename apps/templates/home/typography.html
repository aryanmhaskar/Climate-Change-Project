Our Warming World: The problem of carbon emissions

The Issue The world is ending. Or at least, it’s on its way there. Humans are ever-hungry for energy. Whether for transportation, lighting, farming, electricity, or more, we can’t get enough of it. Unfortunately, the main way to produce energy today is through burning fossil fuels, which releases huge amounts of carbon dioxide: a “greenhouse gas.” This means our atmosphere is trapping more and more heat, warming the global climate at an increasing pace every year. This has disastrous consequences for the water supply, ecosystems, weather phenomena, ocean levels, and human development.
